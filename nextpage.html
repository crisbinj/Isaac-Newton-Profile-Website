Isaac Newton's Achievements

Scientific Achievements:

  • Formulated the laws of motion
  • Discovered the law of universal gravitation
  • Invented calculus independently
  • Studied optics and formulated the theory of color
  • Contributed to the development of calculus

Mathematical Achievements:

  • Developed the binomial theorem
  • Contributed to the understanding of infinite series

Other Notable Achievements:

  • Served as Master of the Royal Mint
  • Wrote extensively on various subjects including theology and alchemy
